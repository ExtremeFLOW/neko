pde_filter Module Reference

A PDE based filter.

type  pde_filter_t
 A PDE based filter mapping $\rho \mapsto \tilde{\rho}$, see Lazarov & O. Sigmund 2010, by solving an equation of the form $\f -r^2 \nabla^2 \tilde{\rho} + \tilde{\rho} = \rho . More...
